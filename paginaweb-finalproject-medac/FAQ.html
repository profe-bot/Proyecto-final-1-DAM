Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


General Questions

What is Coastline?

Coastline is a free-for-all tabletop card game set during the bronze age in the mediterranean and middle east, different cultures explore new lands, discover new technologies and fight over resources.


How can I Play?

Currently Coastline is in beta and this site will provide download links for printable versions of the game in the near future! A full game manual can be found here (insert link to game manual here)


What is this site for?

The coastline site hopes to solve all your deck building and card database needs, providing a comprehensive search tool and a deck creation tool for registered users. Registration is needed to acess all the sites features, but searching can be done without an account.

Your account

How do I create an account?

Introduce your email and a password to the registry form and you can have your account running as soon as we process your request.


What functionalities does an account grant me?

An account allows you to acess the deck creation tool (creating, editing, importing and exporting decks) and the game log tool so you may store information about the games of coastline you have played.


How can I change my account password?

Click on the "I have forgotten my password" button in the log in screen, you should recieve an email with the password change form, this will require you to log in again. If you do not recieve an email contact us at coastlinecontactus@gmail.com

Advanced search

Name search

Introduce a word or string of letters that appears in the name of the card, all cards with that string present in the name will be displayed.


Text search

Introduce a word or string of letters that appear in the text of the card, all cards with that string present in the text will be displayed.


Type search

Filters cards by type.


Gold cost search

Introduce a number to find cards that have an equal, greater to or lesser than gold cost to the given number.


Culture cost search

Introduce any number of culture cost simbols:
"I" for Iberians.
"C" for Celts.
"E" for Etruscans.
"M" for Myceneans.
"P" for Phoenicians.
"H" for Hittites.
And all cards with that given culture cost will be displayed.


Culture filter

Filter cards by their cultural identity


Power/toughness search

Introduce a number to find cards that have an equal, greater to or lesser than power or toughness to the given number.


Set filter

Filter cards by the set they were released in by using their set code.


Search by ID

Search cards by their unique ID code.


Deck creation tool

What is a deck?

A deck is a collection of cards that are assigned to a user in a specific configuration.


How do I create a deck?

By clicking the create deck button a new deck will be created for your user.


How do i add cards to/remove cards from a deck?

Just type in the name of the card you want to add in the name field and the ammount in the ammount field.


How do i delete a deck?

Just click on the delete deck button in the deck editor and your deck will be removed from your list of decks.


How do i import a deck?

Importing a deck requires a txt file with the names and ammounts of the cards in plain text, separated by commas. Click the import deck button and upload the .txt file and the site will copy its information to the deck you are editing.


How do i export a deck?

Click on the export deck button to download your deck in a .txt file detailing all the cards you have added to it and their quantities.


Game log

What is a Game log?

A game log is a small compilation of data you introduce about a game of Coastline you have played, it details the number of players, the decks they used, the ammount of turns played, the winning player, the winning deck and comments.


How do I create a Game log?

By clicking the log game button you will create an empty game log, then just fill it in with your data and it will be saved to our server.


How do i edit a Game log?

Select the game log by clicking on it and choose the edit log function, this will let you edit the data you inputed and add new data.


How do i delete a Game log?

Select the game log by clicking on it and choose the delete log function.
